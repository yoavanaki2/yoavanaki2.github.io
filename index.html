Yala solves the critical problems that every business deals with

It's the easiest way to get original human content published on all your social media accounts.

Your business' social media presence requires constant upkeep: coming up with great content to share.
Sunday through Saturday, people want to hear from your business - and you want to keep the friendly conversation going.

The problem is, coming up with brand new content every single day is hard. Even the strongest storytellers struggle to flex their creative muscles day in, day out.

That's where Yala comes in.

She gets more people in your team involved in social media. Every weekday, Yala messages one of your teammates to ask: what should I share today?

Your teammates send their posts to Yala easily, in Slack. To make sure everything is a-okay, Yala will ask you for permission to share the post she's received.

Once you've approved the post, it is added to your social media queue - right in Slack - and Yala publishes it at the perfect time.
